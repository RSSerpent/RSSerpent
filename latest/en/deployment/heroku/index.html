    Deploy with Heroku¶

    Deploying an RSSerpent instance on Heroku is easy & free! You will need a GitHub and a Heroku account before proceeding.

    1. Go to the RSSerpent Heroku deployment template, then click the green Use this template button. You will be prompted to create a repository based on the template.
    2. After successful creation, in your generated repository, click the purple Deploy to Heroku button. You will be redirected to Heroku and then be prompted to fill in you Heroku app name. Wait for minutes and your RSSerpent instance will be up & running!
